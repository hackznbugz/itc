Catalyzing Vision into Action: A Personal Message from Our Managing Director.

MD MESSAGE

Gratitude towards the Government

It is with great honour that I, as the Managing Director of Al-Attas Group in Saudi Arabia, take this opportunity to greet all our esteemed Clients.

We are immensely proud to be part of Kingdome Developments and extend our deepest gratitude towards the Government, clients and contractors for providing us with such wonderful opportunities. We also pledge our commitment towards supporting the Saudi 2030 vision which has been launched by His Majesty King Salman bin Abdulaziz Al Saud in 2016 for transforming Saudi Arabia into a hub of economic development through various initiatives such as diversifying its economy away from oil dependence and creating jobs for its citizens among others.

Our company is dedicated to upholding these values while striving hard to ensure success in every venture we undertake by delivering high-quality services at competitive prices within stipulated timelines so that it can contribute significantly towards achieving these goals set out under Vision 2030 plan. We believe that together we can create a better future not only for ourselves but also generations yet unborn who will reap benefits from this initiative taken up today!
