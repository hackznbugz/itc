SAUDI VISION 2030

Transformative journey

In alignment with Saudi Arabia's visionary goals outlined in Vision 2030, our telecommunications business is dedicated to playing a pivotal role in the Kingdom's transformative journey. Vision 2030, spearheaded by Crown Prince Mohammed bin Salman, envisions a diversified and vibrant economy, a thriving society, and a technologically advanced nation. As we embark on numerous projects within the Kingdom, we are committed to contributing to the growth and development outlined in Vision 2030. Through cutting-edge telecommunications solutions, innovative technologies, and strategic partnerships, we aim to be a catalyst for progress, enhancing connectivity and communication infrastructure to empower Saudi Arabia's economic and social evolution. Our endeavors are not just a testament to our commitment to excellence but also a reflection of our dedication to being a key player in the realization of Vision 2030, fostering a prosperous and sustainable future for the Kingdom.
