ABOUT THE COMPANY

We Industrial Technology Co. Ltd

A Subsidiary Of Al-Attas Group

ITC is a certified corporation with ISO accreditation, operating as a subsidiary of the Al-Attas Group in Saudi Arabia. Our diverse portfolio encompasses interests in ELV telecommunication systems, power and electrical, as well as mechanical components. We proudly offer a comprehensive range of industrial and electrical materials.

In collaboration with our esteemed partners and alliances, ITC is positioned to deliver highly technical and competitive capabilities. This is particularly crucial in a telecommunications supply market that demands innovative and flexible execution approaches. Our goal is to provide industrial investors with distinctive services that foster successful and sustainable businesses. The proficiency of our highly experienced and specialized staff ensures the delivery of high quality and effective services

A notable facet of our offerings is ITC Calibration Services, distinguished by top-class quality systems. ITC CALIBRATION SERVICES ensures precision, reliability, and promptness in calibration services. Instrument calibration involves a meticulous comparison to standards maintained by our ISO 9001:2008 certified ITC CALIBRATION laboratory, following ISO/IEC 17025-2005 Certified Procedures. We are unwavering in our commitment to meeting the quality requirements of both internal and external customers, striving to provide the best possible service.

Mission statement

Registered and licensed in Kingdom with the aim of building a strong foundation as a commercial contractor throughout the nation. Considering the current industrial growth in the region, we recognize the need for more reliable and capable service organizations.

ITE’s vision is “To further strengthen and expand our new established position, and continue being the first choice for Clients and Prospective Customers”. ITE’s mission is “To serve its customers with quality services which add value to their projects”. Our vision is the conduit for this success, and is founded on a teambuilding approach to our employee and client relationships. This Vision encompasses the tradition of quality services with an emphasis on innovation. Integral to the concept of quality is our adherence to the ethical and professional standards. We’ll strive to implement long term relationship with our clients, based on safety, quality, timely service and an anticipation of their needs.
